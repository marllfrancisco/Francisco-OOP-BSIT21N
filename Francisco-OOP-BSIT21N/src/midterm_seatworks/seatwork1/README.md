## Reflection
At first, tracing the values of variables was confusing; I had to clarify to my peers and to my professor how to trace them accurately. I marked in my mind that double ampersand, which is logical AND, does not 
check the second argument when the first is already false: for efficiency. Similar also happens in logical OR, double pipe: when first condition is true, the expression is evaluated as true.
On the other hand, bitwise AND and OR operate on both arguments. This is my foundation for 
solving this activity. I am delighted that I got a perfect score announced in Canvas.
